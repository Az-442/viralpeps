## What You Need to Record (Checklist per Day)

**For each video you'll need:**
1. Laptop with ViralPeps open (Chrome/Edge)
2. Phone camera ready to film the screen
3. Script printed or on a second device to read
4. Steady hand — rest phone against something if needed

**Recording tips:**
- Film in landscape, then crop to 9:16 in editing (or hold phone vertically while filming laptop)
- Natural finger scrolling looks better than smooth cursor movement
- Pause briefly on key elements (price, supplier name) so viewers can read
- Speak clearly, slightly louder than normal conversation
- If you mess up, just restart the sentence — don't overthink it
- First 2-3 takes will feel awkward, by video 4 it becomes natural

**File naming:** Save each MP4 as `vp-week1-day[N]-[topic].mp4` and send here.

### B2C Protocol Service (Research Use Only)
A service where users submit details (goals, weight, height, experience level) and receive a personalised research protocol framework — compounds to study, dosages, cycle length, stacking suggestions. Priced per protocol or subscription.

*Legal: Must be framed as "for research/educational purposes only. Not medical advice." No diagnosing, no prescribing. Published research summaries applied to user parameters. A foreign company doesn't protect you — MHRA enforces against UK-facing services. Cleanest: run as a separate company that advertises on ViralPeps, with its own terms and disclaimers.*

### B2C Recurring Models
- Subscription (£20-50/mo) — ongoing protocol adjustments, check-ins, new research
- "Peptide Cycling Calendar" — paid quarterly plan updates
- Group coaching — cheaper per person, higher volume
- Lab report interpretation — they send COAs, you explain them monthly

### AI Research Assistant (Case Study Model)
Paid subscription where user submits a hypothetical research scenario (subject profile, injury/goal). AI maps it to published research in our DB, provides preferred supplier + discount code. Ongoing monthly value: fresh research updates, advanced stacking, COA verification, scenario variations.

**Free taster → paid upsell funnel:**
1. **Free tier:** User types any research question → AI returns a short summary (3-5 bullet points) with source links. Just enough to prove value. No supplier links, no protocol depth. Captures email.
2. **Paid tier (£15-25/mo):** Full research map, dosing ranges from literature, stacking suggestions, preferred supplier + discount code, monthly new-research updates, COA interpretation. Everything above the free taster line.
3. **Premium tier (£30-40/mo):** Everything in paid + scenario variation comparisons, cycling plans, community access, contributor status with anonymous case study library.
4. **Personalised tier (£50-75/mo, premium upsell):** User provides actual personal data (body weight, age, health markers, goals) → AI generates a fully personalised research framework. Higher risk, higher price. Strongest legal / medical disclaimers required. Offer as optional add-on after proving value in lower tiers.

**6-month research cycle (implicit — never called out):**
- Month 1: Research assessment + full research map + Week 1-4 plan + supplier discount
- Month 5: User submits dosage adjustment info → AI refines, generates Week 5-10 protocol adjustments
- Month 10: User submits second checkpoint for Week 11-16 refinement
- Month 16: Week 17-22 refinement
- Month 22: Final weeks + research conclusion asked
- Month 26: "Your research is complete. Submit your findings to help us build the database."
- No "6 month plan" mentioned to user. They just receive ongoing value and naturally submit feedback at checkpoints.

**Lifetime discount code upsell (£4.99/month):**
- Users on paid tier can add £4.99/mo for lifetime unlimited supplier discount codes
- Not tied to the subscription — if they cancel the research AI, the discount codes stop too
- Keeps the "lifetime" label attractive but the subscription lock-in protects margin
- Alternatively: £49 one-time "lifetime discount pass" with unlimited codes — simpler, no recurring billing, but no retention hook

### Pharmacy Comparison Site (Affiliate)
Separate site from ViralPeps — compare UK online pharmacies by treatment (weight loss, hair loss, sexual health, etc.). Affiliate model: link out to registered pharmacies, earn commission on sales. No GPhC license needed — not a pharmacy, just a comparison/affiliate site. Same Next.js architecture as ViralPeps, launch fast. Low-difficulty keywords available at treatment level. Proven UK model: PharmaMate.co.uk.
